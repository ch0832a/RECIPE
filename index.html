Alfajores

Combine all the ingredients in the blender, with the kneading hook. Once it looks that it is easy to knead, remove from the blender and stretch with a rolling pin. Cut with a round mold. Bake at 300 F for about 8 minutes. It should not look golden, just well cooked. When cold, fill them with dulce de leche and cover the cookies with powdered sugar. .
